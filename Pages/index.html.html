Yin Yoga Sound Journey: with Aubrey Barker, Kiranjot Kaur & MeiRòn Egger!
 
A 2-Hour Deep-Dive within
 
Offer yourself the gift of embarking on a Yin Yoga Sound Journey, where deep rest meets devotional sound.
 
Guided by Aubrey, you’ll slowly move through a series of long-held, yin postures designed to cultivate expansive breath and a grounding force within. As you surrender into each shape, Kiranjot Kaur and MeiRòn will gift us with live-improvised, soul-nourishing music; weaving together harmonium, flute, guitar, and voice in a cocoon of healing resonance.
 
This is an opportunity to unwind the tension held in the body and soften into the breath—offering space for emotions to surface, be witnessed, and move through. There is nothing to force, nothing to fix—only an invitation to root into yourself and be with what arises.
 
We will begin and end in stillness, anchored by meditation and breath, inviting you into deeper layers of surrender and inner peace.
